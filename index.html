About

As a professional with a BSc in Computer Science with Artificial Intelligence, my journey in the tech industry has been one of continuous growth, learning, and adaptation. Since graduating, I have had the privilege of working in Saudi Arabia, where I have honed my skills as a developer and expanded my expertise in areas like data engineering, web development, and data visualization. My career trajectory has allowed me to evolve from a recent graduate to a well-rounded professional contributing to impactful projects.

Over the years, I have developed technical proficiency in a diverse range of tools and technologies. In addition to foundational skills in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I have worked extensively with modern frameworks and libraries such as **Node.js**, **TypeScript**, **Tailwind CSS**, and **Angular** for building robust and efficient web applications. My experience extends to **ASP.NET Core**, where I’ve created secure and scalable RESTful APIs, as well as **Flutter**, enabling me to design and implement cross-platform mobile applications. Additionally, I have worked with **SQL** and advanced database handling, reinforcing my ability to develop data-driven solutions.

Working in Saudi Arabia has taught me invaluable lessons about adaptability, collaboration, and the importance of understanding the cultural and business contexts of my projects. My role in developing dashboards and data visualization tools for the National Labor Observatory has deepened my expertise in leveraging data to empower decision-making and deliver solutions with a lasting impact. From integrating complex data systems to automating processes, I’ve embraced challenges that have pushed me to grow both technically and personally.

Beyond my technical capabilities, I have developed strong project management and problem-solving skills. Whether facilitating workshops, designing user-friendly interfaces, or collaborating with cross-functional teams, I’ve cultivated a strong work ethic and a passion for continuous improvement. These experiences have shaped me into a developer who understands not only the technical intricacies of software development but also the human element that drives innovation.

Today, I am more committed than ever to leveraging my skills and experiences to contribute to innovative projects that create value. As I continue this journey, I look forward to connecting with like-minded professionals and organizations that share my passion for technology, growth, and meaningful impact. Thank you for taking the time to learn more about my professional journey.
